About

Rushtaurant Secrets is a top down 2D kitchen simulator game. You play as a young, ambitious girl that has recently become orphaned. After your parent's deaths under mysterious circumstances, you decide to reopen your parent's old business, an old restaurant. Soon after the reopening, you discover the reason why the restaurant was clased in the first place. The rival restaurants in the area are trying to steal the restaurant's source of success: the secret formula!

Will you be able to stop your greedy competitors?
