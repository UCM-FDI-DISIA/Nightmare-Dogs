About

Rushtaurant Secrets is a top down 2D kitchen simulator game. You play as a young, ambitious girl that has recently become orphaned. After your parent's deaths under mysterious circumstances, you decide to reopen their old business, an old restaurant. Soon after the reopening, you discover the reason why the restaurant was closed in the first place. The rival businesses in the area are trying to steal the restaurant's source of success: the secret formula!

Will you be able to stop your greedy competitors?

Find out yourself!

How to play

1 - Choose today's menu

At the start of every day, you'll be able to choose one of the two randomly generated menus. Choose which one you want carefully since you won't be able to change it for the day.

2 - Buy the ingredients

After choosing your menu, you'll have to buy the ingredients you need. Ingredients expire at the end of the day.

  1. Find the ingredient in the Supermarket
  2. Interact with the item
  3. Go to the cash register
  4. Pay (make sure you have enough money!)

3 - Seat your customers

During your opening hours, customers will start coming to your restaurant. It is time to seat them!

4 - Cook the food

After your customer makes their order, it is time to cook

  1. Grab the ingredients you need
  2. Interact with the stove
  3. Wait for the stove to cook
  4. Take the dish to the customer

5 - Serve your customers

You can serve the customer their dish after you finish cooking it

  1. Take the dish to the customer
  2. Wait for them to finish eating
  3. Charge them at the cash register

Tip: You'll get more money the larger the amount of customers you charge at once is

Spoiler

After your first day, thieves will start appearing at your pantry. Make sure to stop them before they reach the secret formula!
